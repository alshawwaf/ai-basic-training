The Unified Pitch (2 Minutes)

  1. The Landscape (20s) — "AI adoption is accelerating in three areas: employees using AI tools, orgs building AI apps, and agents automating operations."
  2. The Risk (30s) — "Employees leak data to ChatGPT. LLM apps are vulnerable to prompt injection. Agents have permissions without human approval."
  3. The Solution (30s) — "Check Point AI Security covers all three. Workforce gives visibility. Guardrails protect apps. Agent Security monitors autonomous workflows."
  4. The Differentiator (20s) — "Other vendors cover one — we cover all three, integrated into the Infinity Platform."
  5. The Proof (20s) — "Let me show you. I have a working demo."
