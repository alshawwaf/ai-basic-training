Cybersecurity tie-in: Real BPE tokenisers never throw away your text — but rare strings like SHA-256 hashes, IPs, and CVE IDs get split into many subword pieces. One log line can consume 50+ tokens, so when wiring an LLM into a security pipeline, count the tokens before you count the bills.
